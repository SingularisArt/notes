\lesson{4}{Sep 13 2021 Mon (10:54:11)}{Properties of Matter}

There are four known states of matter:

\begin{itemize}
  \item Solid
  \item Liquid
  \item Gas
  \item Plasma
\end{itemize}

\begin{definition}[Phase]
  Each one of these stages is known as a \textbf{Phase}. Each one has it's own properties, such as:

  \begin{itemize}
    \item Shape
    \item Particle Motion
  \end{itemize}
\end{definition}
