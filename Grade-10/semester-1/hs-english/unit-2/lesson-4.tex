\subsubsection*{Different types of conflict}

\paragraph{Character vs. Character} This type of conflict is between at least two characters.
\paragraph{Character vs. Nature} This type of conflict occurs between a character and some element of nature (wind, rain, storms, animals, etc.).
\paragraph{Character vs. Society} This type of conflict occurs between the protagonist and his or her society.
\paragraph{Character vs. Self} This type of conflict occurs within a person and might take the form of a psychological struggle.
