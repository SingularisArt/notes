\lesson{3}{Sep 15 2021 Wed (13:05:23)}{Connotation, Denotation, and Imagery}{Unit 1}

Words and images get their power from \textbf{Connotation}. Most words have
both connotative and denotative meaning.

We refer to the author's choice of words as diction. Diction is the foundation
for writing. It impacts other writing tolls including imager, detail, and
figurative language.
