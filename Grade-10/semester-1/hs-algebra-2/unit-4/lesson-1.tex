\begin{definition}[Rational Expression]
  \bf{Rational Expressions} are just expressions with a polynomial in the
  numerator and the denominator.

  Here are some examples:

  \begin{align}
    \frac{5x + 35}{5x} \\
    \frac{x^2 - 6x - 27}{x - 9}
  .\end{align}
\end{definition}
