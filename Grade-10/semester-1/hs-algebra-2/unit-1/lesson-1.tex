Here are the basic steps to simplifying a radical expression:

\begin{property}[Radical Expressions]
    \begin{align}
    	t^{\frac{3}{4}} &= t^{\frac{3}{4}} \times t^{\frac{3}{4}} \times t^{\frac{3}{4}} \\
                    	&= \sqrt[4]{t^3} \\
                    	&= \sqrt[4]{t} \times \sqrt[4]{t} \times \sqrt[4]{t} \\
                    	&= t^{\frac{3}{4}} \\
                    	&= \sqrt[4]{t^3} \\
    \end{align}
\end{property}
And here are the basic steps to simplifying a rational expression:

\begin{property}[Rational Expressions]
    \begin{align}
    	\sqrt[5]{x^3} &= \sqrt[5]{x} \times \sqrt[5]{x} \times \sqrt[5]{x} \\
                      &= x^{\frac{3}{5}} \\
                      &= x^{\frac{1}{5}} \times x^{\frac{1}{5}} \times x^{\frac{1}{5}} \\
                      &= x^{\frac{3}{5}} \\
                      &= \sqrt[5]{x^3} \\
    \end{align}
\end{property}
