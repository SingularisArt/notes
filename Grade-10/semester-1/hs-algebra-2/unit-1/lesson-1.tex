\subsubsection*{Rational Exponents become $\rightarrow$ Radical Expressions}

The \bf{numerator} of the \bf{rational exponent} becomes the \bf{exponent} on the \bf{radicand}.
The \bf{denominator} of the \bf{rational exponent} becomes the \bf{index}, or \bf{root}, of the \bf{radical}.

\begin{align}
    t^{\frac{3}{4}} = \sqrt[4]{t^3}
\end{align}

\subsubsection*{Radical Expressions become $\rightarrow$ Rational Exponents}

The \bf{exponent} on the \bf{radicand} becomes the \bf{numerator} of the \bf{rational exponent}. The \bf{index}, or \bf{root}, of the \bf{radical} becomes the \bf{denominator} of the \bf{rational exponent}.

\begin{align}
    \sqrt[8]{w^5} = w^{\frac{5}{8}}
\end{align}
