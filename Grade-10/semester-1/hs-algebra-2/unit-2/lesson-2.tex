\subsubsection*{Factoring by Grouping (Four-Term Polynomials) }

Let's just jump into an example:

\begin{example}[Factor $30c^3 - 20c^2 - 15c + 10$]
    \begin{align}
        30c^2 - 20c^2 -15c + 10 &= 5(6c^3 - 4c^2 - 3c + 2) \\
                                &= 5[2c^2 (3c - 2) - 1 (3c - 2)] \\
                                &= 5[2c^2 (3c - 2) - 1 = (3c - 2)] \\
                                &= 5(3c - 2)(2c^2 - 1)
    \end{align}
\end{example}
