\subsubsection{Find the Axis of Symmetry}
\label{sub_sub_sec:find_the_axis_of_symmetry}

The axis of symmetry is the line that divides the parabola into two mirror
images.

% subsubsection find_the_axis_of_symmetry (end)

\subsubsection{Find the Vertex}
\label{sub_sub_sec:find_the_vertex}

If the parabola opens upward, the vertex will be the lowest point.

If the parabola opens downward, the vertex will be the highest point.

\begin{note}
  \label{nte:find_the_vertex}

  The vertex always lies on the axis of symmetry.
\end{note}

% subsubsection find_the_vertex (end)
