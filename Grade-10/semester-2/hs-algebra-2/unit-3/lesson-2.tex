\lesson{2}{Mar 18 2022 Fri (16:53:18)}{Power/product/quotient rules}{Unit 3}
\label{les_2_unit_3:power_product_quotient_rules}

Let’s simplify the following item. It has all of the rules we will go over:

\begin{equation}
  \label{spt:power_product_quotient_rules}
  \begin{split}
    (-2u^{3}v^{2}w^{-3})^{-3} \left(\frac{u^{-1}}{w^{3}}\right)
    &= \frac{1}{(-2u^{3}v^{2}w^{-3})} \frac{1}{u^{1}w^{3}} \\
    &= \frac{1}{(-2)^{3}u^{9}v^{6}w^{3}} \times \frac{1}{u^{1}w^{3}} \\
    &= \frac{1}{-8u^{10}v^{6}w^{6}} \\
    &= - \frac{1}{8u^{10}v^{6}w^{6}} \\
  \end{split}
.\end{equation}
