% How does an understanding of natural rights and respect build a free society?

A society whose citizens don't understand their natural rights and have respect
is bound to fall sooner or later. But, if the citizens understand their
natural rights and have respect for each other, not only will the society
be stable and safe, but it will gradually grow. The affects of having the
citizens understand their rights are just as important as actually having the
rights because if they aren't aware of their rights, the rights might as well,
be existent.
