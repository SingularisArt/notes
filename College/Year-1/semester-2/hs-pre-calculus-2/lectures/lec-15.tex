\lesson{15}{May 21 2022 Sat (22:19:18)}{Double-Angle and Half-Angle Identities}

In this chapter, we'll find identities that will allow us to calculate $\sin
(2\theta)$ and $\cos (2\theta)$ if we know the values of $\sin (\theta)$ and
$\cos (\theta`)$ (we call these "double-angle identities") and we will find
identities that will allow us to calculate $\sin (\frac{\theta}{2})$ and $\cos
(\frac{\theta}{2})$. (we call these "half-angle identities").
